Anaerobic capacity / MAOD (screen 1)

 

The MOAD test is a short-duration, high-intensity activity. In screen 1, values from the activity are entered as O2 consumed and workrate the activity was performed at (either km/h or watts), in screen two they are entered as O2 consumed and the time intervals of the activity (in seconds).

The module them calculates the relationship between O2­ consumed and workload performed in screen one, providing a linear equation based on regression. In screen two, the required workload to reach a provided VO2max and the corresponding MAOD is calculated.
